# Risk Register – Small Business Environment

| Risk ID | Risk Description | Likelihood (1–5) | Impact (1–5) | Risk Score | Mitigation Strategy | Owner | Status |
|--------|------------------|------------------|--------------|------------|----------------------|--------|--------|
| R-001 | Ransomware attack on employee laptops | 3 | 5 | 15 | Implement endpoint protection, enable automatic backups, conduct security awareness training | IT Manager | Open |
| R-002 | Customer device lost or stolen during intake | 2 | 4 | 8 | Use locked storage, device tracking log, restricted access | Store Lead | Monitoring |
| R-003 | Website outage preventing customer appointments | 3 | 3 | 9 | Migrate to a reliable cloud host, implement uptime monitoring | Web Admin | Open |
| R-004 | Employee falls for phishing attack | 4 | 4 | 16 | Enforce MFA, conduct quarterly phishing training, email filtering | Security Team | Open |
| R-005 | Data breach exposing customer PII | 2 | 5 | 10 | Encrypt database, apply least privilege access, conduct monthly audit | IT Manager | Open |
| R-006 | Power outage interrupts business operations | 3 | 3 | 9 | Use backup power supply, ensure cloud backups, emergency procedures | Operations Lead | Closed |
| R-007 | POS system fails during peak hours | 3 | 4 | 12 | Maintain redundant system, regularly update POS hardware/software | Store Lead | Open |
| R-008 | Insider threat leading to data misuse | 2 | 5 | 10 | Implement access logs, role-based access control, employee vetting | HR + IT Security | Monitoring |
| R-009 | Malware infection from customer devices | 4 | 3 | 12 | Isolate devices during diagnostic, use sandboxing tools | Repair Technicians | Open |
| R-010 | Unauthorized access to Wi-Fi network | 3 | 4 | 12 | Enforce WPA3, separate guest network, rotate passwords | Network Engineer | Open |
