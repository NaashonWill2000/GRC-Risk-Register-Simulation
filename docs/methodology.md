# Risk Assessment Methodology

## Likelihood Scale (1–5)
1 – Rare  
2 – Unlikely  
3 – Possible  
4 – Likely  
5 – Almost Certain  

## Impact Scale (1–5)
1 – Negligible  
2 – Minor  
3 – Moderate  
4 – Major  
5 – Critical (financial loss, legal consequences)

## Risk Score
Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact

## Mitigation Approach
- Avoid
- Transfer
- Mitigate
- Accept
